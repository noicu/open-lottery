About Australia's Official Lotteries





New look for the Lott!

You may have noticed we’ve changed our logo, but we’re still the official home of Australia’s lotteries.
While our logo is changing, it’s important to note that there are no changes to our lottery games, pricing, membership or how you claim prizes. We’ll continue to inspire dreams, deliver wins, and bring you the games you love. Keep an eye out for our new logo rolling out across our 3,800 retailers Australia-wide. We love our new logo, and hope you do too! Learn more

The Lott is the mark of trust that unites Australia's Official Lotteries by Tatts (Tattersall's Sweeps Pty Ltd), Tatts NT (Tatts NT Lotteries Pty Ltd), NSW Lotteries (NSW Lotteries Corporation Pty Ltd), Golden Casket (Golden Casket Lottery Corporation Limited) and SA Lotteries (Tatts Lotteries SA Pty Ltd) under one banner.

Every time you play your favourite games through Australia's Official Lotteries, you are helping to play an important role in supporting and inspiring our Australian communities.

Our Commitment

The global authority on lotteries, the World Lottery Association (WLA) is a member-based organisation committed to supporting its members to achieve the highest standards of corporate responsibility and responsible gambling principles. At the Lott, Australia’s official lotteries, we have adopted these principles and achieved the WLA’s highest level of certification (Level 4).

Responsible gambling is not a new concept to us and we continue to ensure responsible practices are integrated into day-to-day activities so that our lottery games are operated, marketed and sold in a socially responsible manner. To maintain this accreditation, the Lott must demonstrate, with evidence, our commitment to the continuous improvement of our responsible gambling practices. In this way, we maintain our focus on providing our customers with a safe, secure and friendly environment in which to play our games.
